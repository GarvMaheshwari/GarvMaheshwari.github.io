Overview

This application is a full-stack mobile app that was designed with the intended purpose of tracking volunteer hours for a student organization at my alma mater high school. Designed and implemented using Object Oriented Programming, Dart, Flutter framework, Firebase Firestore NoSQL database and Firebase Auth.

Situation

My client, the advisor of the organization, was overwhelmed with keeping track of 100+ members student volunteer logs. My client desired a mobile platform on which students could log their volunteer hours in the hopes of easing his burden of manually sifting through 100 student volunteer hour logs.

Solution

Over the course of 10 weeks I interviewed, iteratively designed, and presented a potential app solution to my client. To ensure I met my clientâ€™s needs and truly understood them, I conducted numerous interviews throughout the project lifecycle. This app required me to make use of a NoSQL database, authentication service and work on both Android and IOS devices. At the time, I had not yet experienced using these tools or design philosophies, so I had to research and discover how I could best meet my clients needs. After delivering the product to the client, he shared how excited he was to no longer have to sift through over a hundred students' logs.
