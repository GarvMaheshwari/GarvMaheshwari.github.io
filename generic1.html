Price Match Product Comparison App

Overview

This application is a web app that was designed with the intended purpose of tracking product prices across multiple online retailers. Implemented using Object Oriented Programming, Dart, Flutter framework, Firebase, Retail REST APIs, and Firebase Auth.

Situation

In the aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic movement of people and businesses transformed. My client, an avid shopper, found himself burdened by the number of retail websites he needed to visit to ensure he was getting the best price.

Solution

Over the course of 6 weeks I interviewed, iteratively designed, and presented a web app. To ensure I met my client's needs I conducted a series of interviews throughout the project lifecycle. After determining the requirements and constraints of the project, I truly realized what the scope of the project required of me: an understanding of REST APIs. After numerous iterations, I was able to present a web app that made use of REST APIs from popular online retailers. The Price Match Product Comparison App served as an answer to my client's desired problem of having to visit multiple online retailer websites to compare product prices.
